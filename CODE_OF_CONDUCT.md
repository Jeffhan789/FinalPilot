# Code of Conduct

FinalPilot is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community. Be respectful, assume good intent, give actionable feedback, and avoid discriminatory, threatening, sexualized, or personally identifying content.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and handled proportionately.

This policy applies in repository discussions, pull requests, issues, and project-related community spaces.
